Our Story

Founded in 2015, MoneyMap has grown from a small startup to a leading global payment platform trusted by businesses in over 200 countries. Our mission is to make cross-border payments simple, secure, and affordable for everyone.

We believe that every business, regardless of size or location, should have access to powerful financial tools that help them grow. That's why we've built a platform that combines cutting-edge technology with exceptional customer service.

Today, we process billions of dollars in transactions annually and serve thousands of satisfied customers. But we're just getting started. We're constantly innovating and expanding our services to meet the evolving needs of modern businesses.
